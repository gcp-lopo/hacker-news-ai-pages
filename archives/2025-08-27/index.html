New Sni5Gect Attack Crashes Phones and Downgrades 5G to 4G without Rogue Base Station

‚ÄúThe state information is then used to inject a targeted attack payload in downlink communication.‚Äù The findings build upon a prior study from ASSET in late 2023 that led to the discovery of 14 flaws in the firmware implementation of 5G mobile network modems from MediaTek and Qualcomm, collectively dubbed 5Ghoul , that could be exploited to launch attacks to drop connections, freeze the connection that involves manual reboot, or downgrade the 5G connectivity to 4G.
